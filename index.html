Hi. I'm John.

I like to draw, design, and draft. I work driven by a narrative (e.g., educational, analytical, etc.) and I want that to show in whatever project I am a part of. My goal is to further cultivate my skills as an illustrator, designer, writer, and editor. Currently, I work as a technical writer and editor. I'm comfortable with highly technical subject matter, particularly that which is grounded in biological and medical sciences. I have experience conveying complex concepts and cultivating comprehension to and for less subject-savvy audiences.
